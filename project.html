1vs1 Puzzle project

About the project

In this case, the game mechanics are easy. You and another player will face each other in an online duel to see who is the fastest to complete the puzzle that is chosen for you. Between the two of you, you will decide how many pieces you want it to have. Whoever does the puzzle first wins and gets points to save it in a database to show the players with the highest score of the month.

The advantages of this game are multiple. They help us develop coordination, motor skills, laterality, improve observation skills and force the brain to work both hemispheres. In addition, there are traditional puzzles, 3D puzzles and Rubik's cubes, among others.

Basket Simulator (beta) project

About the project

As the name says, the game focuses on a basket simulator. Thanks to VR we can use this game that will allow us to visualize the game. In addition, thanks to our great game development and programming, we can examine in detail the movement of each game, jump height, shooting speed, etc. It is still in constant development and the possibilities are endless.

In addition to being entertaining for your players, our other goal is to improve motor skills such as reflexes, quickness, and decision making. We not only focus on selling, but on selling something useful and beneficial for everyone. This game is not a substitute for the real one, please don't refrain from the reality.
