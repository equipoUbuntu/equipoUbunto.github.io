Who we are

I am, because you are and we are. The essence of our organizational culture is the spirit of belongingness.

Appsmatu S.A. is a software and product development company that helps enterprises with digital transformation and start-ups.

Our goal is clear: we translate ideas into real time solutions and opportunities, always trying to build a culture of innovation at scale. Appsmatu is committed to innovating, with transparency, even in times that people’s needs are constantly changing worldwide.

What about an algorithm to read other people’s minds? As you already know, we translate ideas into reality, and we’ve done something nobody has done before.

The software development team of Appsmatu has devised an algorithm that processes and decodes the words a person hears and thinks about. This is possible undergoing a functional magnetic resonance imaging, an efficient and non-intrusive method.
