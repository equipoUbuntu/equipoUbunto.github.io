Sounds like you’re tired

Stressay is a new technological tool developed by Appsmatu. This tool is able to measure a person’s stress levels in a few minutes by analyzing their voice and allowing them to be viewed on a screen.

Stressay is made with AI technology, which means it has the ability to identify patterns in the acoustic recordings obtained from a person. All you need is to speak into a microphone for 60 seconds in order to catch and measure their words, pauses, breathing and the tone of their voice. Based on this, Stressay is able to read your stress level.

After completing the test, the person receives the results by email, as well as a visual representation of their stress level, which can be found between one of the following five levels: low, low-moderate, moderate, high and super-high.
